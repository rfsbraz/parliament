# Portuguese Parliament Database: Political Research & Analysis Guide

## Overview

This database contains comprehensive data on the Portuguese Parliament (Assembleia da República) spanning from the Constituent Assembly to the XVII Legislature, representing over 45 years of democratic governance. With 236 tables and 1.55+ million records, it provides unprecedented analytical capabilities for Portuguese political research and comparative European parliamentary studies.

**Key Statistics:**
- **6,463** individual deputies across all legislatures
- **20** legislative periods (Constituinte through XVII)
- **20** distinct political parties
- **9** coalition formations
- **848,000+** attendance records
- **119,000+** committee activities
- **57,000+** legislative initiatives

## Data Quality & Methodological Considerations

### Strengths
- **Comprehensive Coverage**: Complete records across 45+ years of democracy
- **High Volume**: Large-N analysis capabilities for robust statistical inference
- **Longitudinal Design**: Time-series analysis of democratic development
- **Multiple Levels**: Individual, party, and institutional-level data integration

### Limitations
- **Data Availability**: Earlier periods may have less detailed records
- **Coding Consistency**: Variable definitions may evolve across legislatures
- **Selection Effects**: Recorded activities may not capture all political behavior
- **External Validity**: Portuguese-specific findings may not generalize

### Best Practices
- **Temporal Controls**: Account for period effects and institutional changes
- **Robustness Checks**: Validate findings across different operationalizations
- **Comparative Framework**: Contextualize Portuguese findings internationally
- **Triangulation**: Combine quantitative analysis with qualitative insights

## Database Table Reference Guide

This section maps each analytical capability to its source tables for technical implementation and back-reference.

### **Core Democratic Infrastructure Tables**

#### **Deputy Analysis**
- **Primary Table**: `deputados` (6,463 records)
  - Fields: `id_cadastro`, `nome`, `nome_completo`, `legislatura_id`, `sexo`, `profissao`, `data_nascimento`, `naturalidade`
  - Usage: Core entity for all deputy-related analysis

- **Supporting Tables**:
  - `deputado_mandatos_legislativos` - Electoral mandates and party affiliations
  - `deputado_habilitacoes` - Educational background
  - `deputado_cargos_funcoes` - Professional positions
  - `deputado_titulos` - Academic and professional titles
  - `deputado_condecoracoes` - Awards and honors
  - `deputado_obras_publicadas` - Publications
  - `deputado_atividades_orgaos` - Organizational activities

#### **Legislative Framework**
- **Primary Table**: `legislaturas` (20 records)
  - Fields: `numero`, `designacao`, `data_inicio`, `data_fim`, `ativa`
  - Usage: Temporal organization and periodization

#### **Political Parties & Coalitions**
- **Primary Tables**:
  - `partidos` (20 records) - Political party master data
  - `coligacoes` (9 records) - Electoral coalitions and alliances
  - `deputado_mandatos_legislativos` - Party membership by legislature

### **Parliamentary Activity Tables**

#### **Legislative Process**
- **Initiative Tracking**:
  - `deputy_initiatives` (57,504 records) - Deputy-authored initiatives
  - `iniciativas_detalhadas` (1,793 records) - Detailed initiative information
  - `iniciativa_autor_deputados` - Initiative authorship links
  - Usage: Legislative productivity and agenda-setting analysis

- **Voting Records**:
  - `orcamento_estado_votacoes` (7,255 records) - Budget voting records
  - `orcamento_estado_grupo_parlamentar_votos` - Party positions on budget votes
  - `atividades_parlamentar_votacao` - General parliamentary voting
  - Usage: Party discipline, coalition behavior, voting pattern analysis

#### **Parliamentary Sessions & Interventions**
- **Primary Tables**:
  - `intervencao_parlamentar` (426 records) - Parliamentary interventions
  - `intervencao_deputados` - Deputy intervention links
  - `organ_meetings` (39,768 records) - Organizational meetings
  - `plenary_compositions` (7,591 records) - Plenary composition data
  - Usage: Parliamentary participation and oversight analysis

### **Performance & Accountability Tables**

#### **Attendance & Participation**
- **Primary Table**: `meeting_attendances` (848,819 records)
  - Fields: `dt_reuniao`, `dep_nome_parlamentar`, `sigla_falta`, `motivo_falta`, `tipo_reuniao`
  - Usage: Deputy performance, participation patterns, institutional health

- **Supporting Tables**:
  - `organ_meetings` - Meeting organization context
  - `deputados` - Deputy identification and legislature links

#### **Committee Activities**
- **Primary Tables**:
  - `actividades_comissao_outs` (119,950 records) - Committee activities across legislatures
  - `actividades_comissao_*` (multiple tables by legislature) - Legislature-specific committee work
  - Usage: Specialization analysis, policy network mapping, oversight effectiveness

#### **Transparency & Ethics**
- **Interest Declarations**:
  - `registo_interesses_v2_*` (10 tables) - Interest declaration records
  - `registo_interesses_unified` - Unified interest tracking
  - Usage: Conflict of interest analysis, elite network mapping

- **Petition System**:
  - `peticoes_*` (12 tables) - Petition processing and responses
  - Usage: Citizen engagement, democratic responsiveness analysis

### **Specialized Analysis Tables**

#### **Budget & Economic Policy**
- **Budget Analysis Tables** (12 tables total):
  - `orcamento_estado_votacoes` - Main budget voting records
  - `orcamento_estado_propostas` - Budget proposals
  - `orcamento_estado_*` - Various budget-related activities
  - Usage: Economic policy analysis, government-opposition dynamics

#### **International Relations**
